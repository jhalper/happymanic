The Studio

25 Years of Making Games

Happy Manic LLC is an independent game development studio founded by Joe Halper, a veteran game developer with over 25 years of experience shipping titles across PC, console, VR, and simulation platforms.

From blockbuster franchises to cutting-edge VR experiences, our team brings deep expertise in game design, production, and immersive technology. We believe games should surprise, challenge, and connect with players on an emotional level.

Based in Raleigh, North Carolina, Happy Manic continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in virtual and mixed reality gaming.
